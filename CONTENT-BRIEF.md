## Positioning Direction
Beaumont & Voss should present itself as an **international boutique property advisory** with flexibility to pursue the right opportunities across markets, rather than being tied to one named area on the homepage.

### Priority audience
- International buyers seeking quality residential opportunities across borders
- Website launch in **English, German, Dutch, and Spanish**
- Buyers seeking local guidance, trusted introductions, and a smoother acquisition process
- Investors and lifestyle buyers who value discretion, clarity, and strong local context

## Refined Value Proposition
**Boutique guidance for international clients buying property in Spain.**

A polished positioning line for the brand:

> **Beaumont & Voss helps international buyers navigate the Spanish property market with local insight, multilingual communication, and discreet, hands-on guidance.**

## Founder / Brand Strengths to Highlight
This is strong and worth using prominently on the homepage:
- English website first, with support for clients in **EN / DE / NL / ES**
- Strong knowledge of the **local market in Castilla y León**
- Established relationships with **local real estate agents and regional contacts**
- Practical understanding of **financing possibilities** for buyers

### Polished version
> Fluent in English, German, Dutch, and Spanish, Beaumont & Voss combines local market understanding with international communication, trusted connections, and practical guidance around acquisition and financing.

## What We Should Use From Your Homepage Input
### Keep
1. **Luxury / premium tone**
   - Strong fit for the intended positioning.
2. **Clear homepage structure**
   - Hero
   - Difference / trust section
   - Featured properties
   - Services
   - Founder / team
   - Contact CTA
3. **Conversion flow**
   - Portfolio → consultation → contact works well.
4. **Lifestyle + investment angle**
   - Good for international buyers choosing Spain for both living and long-term value.

### Adjust
1. **Spain first, not Switzerland first**
   - The copy should now clearly centre on Spain and Castilla y León.
2. **Avoid overclaiming**
   - Phrases like “decades of experience” or “legacy of excellence” should only stay if they are factually true.
3. **More founder-led trust**
   - At launch, the site will feel stronger if it highlights personal language ability, local network, and buyer guidance.

## Tone & Design Guidance
- Premium, calm, elegant
- Not flashy or overly corporate
- Editorial style with clean spacing and refined typography
- Spain should feel aspirational, but still credible and grounded

## Practical Recommendation
The strongest version of the site right now is:
- **Spain-first**
- **Founder-led**
- **Multilingual**
- **Trust and guidance focused**

That gives Beaumont & Voss a clear niche from the start and leaves room to grow internationally later.
